What is Clicking Dog Game?

Clicking Dog Game is a fun and simple clicker game made for everyone who loves dogs! 🐶 You earn clicks and use them to adopt virtual dogs. It's not just a game — it's an experience built to teach coding, creativity, and joy through the magic of interactivity and simplicity.

Why We Made This

We created this game to help beginners learn HTML and web development while also making something playful and rewarding. It’s a great starter project for kids, students, and those who love dogs!
